Why choose Cocovita Oil?

Cocovita oil is extracted from 100% pure coconut flesh with the heat of standard Dryers. Cocovita oil contains no cholesterol, no fibre, no smell and only traces of vitamins, minerals, and plant sterols

Expeller Pressed, Refined & Filtered

The fresh coconut flesh is machine-pressed to release the pure oil. The oil is then heated and filtered to deodorize the oil by filtering through machines to remove impurities and any remaining bacteria
